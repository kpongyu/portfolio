Journey On

Virtual Reality Application
My Role: User Experience Designer, Web Developer
Duration: 3 Months

Check out VR Prototype online

Ideation and scoping

The aim of the study is create a assistance system to help students who have mobility issue temporarily. Including to enhance outdoor and indoor assistive technology and better accessibility, providing safe and customized transport solutions, and helping the students regain self-esteem and social lives.
We start from three concepts, first is a smart mobility assistance device to make "crutch walking" easier. Second is a comprehensive informatics system to help get the health condition of patient in real time. Third is an "extended hand" to create more fun functions for crutches and devices.
We conducted several diary studies to learn more about the daily lives of target students. We learnt the flexibility of time management, self-image, social interaction, and medical advice are of great importance. After analysising through Questions, options and criteria (QOC) process, we designed three unique scenerios, namely transparent on demand crutch, recovery mat, and environment detector, to embed technologies within the daily lives. We designed user enactments to testify the scenerios.

Virtual reality interface design

After a second round QOC, we constraint our design to the recovery tracking space idea, we wants to combine several factors into the system, including recovery tracking, physical therapy, and social connection.We also created a online virtual reality prototype to present how the system would be like.
